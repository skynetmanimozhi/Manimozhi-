About

A disciplined mind with a soft spot for gardens and kolams

I'm Manimozhi, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at RD International College of Arts and Science, Erode. My schooling was completed at Kalaimagal Kalvi Nilayam Girls Higher Secondary School, in the bio-maths group — biology remains my favourite subject, for the simple reason that I never tire of learning how living things work.

Outside the classroom, I'm drawn to app development and game development, and I keep two goals in view at once: building software, and one day serving as a police officer. Away from screens, I draw kolams at the doorstep, tend a small garden, cook, and take long night drives with music on.
